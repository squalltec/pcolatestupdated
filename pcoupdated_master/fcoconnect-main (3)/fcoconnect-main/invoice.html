Cancellation Policy

1.1 Orders cancellation fee - Standard fee

  • 1.1.1 Cancelling 45 days or more before departure: the customer is charged a service fee of AUD 20.
  • 1.1.2 Cancelling between 44 and 21 days before departure: the customer is charged a service fee of AUD 40.

1.2 Orders cancellation fee - Orders with the Timetravels flexible cancellation fee

If you have selected the Timetravels flexible cancellation fee when booking the trip, the cancellation fees are:

  • 1.2.1 Cancelling 45 days or more before departure: the customer is charged a discounted service fee of AUD12.
  • 1.2.2 Cancelling between 44 and 21 days before departure: the customer is charged a discounted service fee of AUD12.
  • 1.2.3 Cancelling between 20 and 7 days before departure: the customer is charged a service fee of 40% of the total order value.
  • 1.2.4a Cancelling between 6 and 3 days before departure: the customer is charged a service fee of 75% of the total order value.

About

In 2009, founder & CEO Dilan Fernando saw the need for a simpler more streamlined, secure and optimal way for Congress organizers to book and manage events. His vision was a simple but powerful one to create a tool for professionals to access, manage, and share event information from anywhere in real time. Not a novel concept today, but it was for the early 2000’s. In those days, no one was using 100% web-based software or “cloud based” as we know it today. PCO Connect not only survived the “dot com” burst of the early 2000s but steadily grew, Today, PCO Connect has serves over 100,000 customers and companies.
